About Pig Dice

Pig is a simple dice game first described in print by John Scarne in 1945.As with many games of folk origin, Pig is played with many rule variations. Commercial variants of Pig include Pass the Pigs, Pig Dice and Skunk. Pig is commonly used by mathematics teachers to teach probability concepts.Pig dice is alsoconsidered a jeopardy game, where you risk everything to see if you can win more.
